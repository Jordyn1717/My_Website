KingsLinks by the Sea Golf Course

KingsLinks is a scenic seaside golf course offering stunning views of the Coast Mountains, Mount Baker, and the Gulf Islands. Known for its unique climate—warmer, drier, and slightly windier than other courses in the Lower Mainland—it provides a year-round golfing experience for its dedicated members. I worked as a server at the golf club where I provided food and drink service for our regulars and members.

Student Health Ambassador at York (SHAY)

SHAY is a volunteer ambassador program for current Faculty of Health students at York University. As a SHAY, I share my experiences in the Health Studies program and as a YorkU student with prospective students and their families. I assist with both online and in-person events, including open houses, the Ontario Universities’ Fair, online program information sessions, and drop-in Q&A sessions.
